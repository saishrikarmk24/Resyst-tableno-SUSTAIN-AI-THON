Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ section for the AquaRegia Web App. Here you'll find answers to common questions about our app.

1. How accurate is satellite-based pollution detection compared to traditional water testing methods?

While not a replacement for chemical testing, our system achieves 85-90% accuracy in detecting visible pollution and can cover vastly larger areas in real-time.

2. What is the minimum size of garbage/pollution that can be detected using satellite imagery?

The system can detect pollution clusters roughly 10 meters or larger in size, depending on satellite resolution and weather conditions.

3. Can I create multiple profiles?

Yes, you can create multple profiles.

4. Can the system differentiate between different types of water pollution?

Yes, the system primarily detects visible surface pollution and turbidity levels.
